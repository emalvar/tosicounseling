About Melisa

Melisa Tosi is an Advanced Practice Social Worker in Wisconsin providing virtual bilingual mental health support for individuals, adolescents, and families.

As an immigrant herself, Melisa brings a culturally informed and compassionate perspective to her work, helping clients from diverse backgrounds feel understood, respected, and supported.

She has experience working with children, adolescents, women, older adults, and individuals with disabilities in both school and community settings.

Melisa provides trauma-informed care in English and Spanish and is currently working toward clinical licensure (LCSW).

APSW, Wisconsin Supervised by Anna Jacobs, LCSW Virtual Sessions Across Wisconsin
